Our Story

Fusion X was founded in 2018 by Alexander N. Kathanzu with a vision to bridge the gap between innovative ideas and practical implementation. What started as a small consultancy has grown into a full-service digital solutions provider serving clients across the globe.

Our name, Fusion X, represents the intersection of technology (X-factor) with business strategy - creating powerful fusion that drives transformation.
